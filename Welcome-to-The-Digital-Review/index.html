Welcome to The Digital Review!

September 11, 2018

Digital media has become a part of our everyday life. We consume content via various digital services every single day, but very little is actually written about the quality, usability and other aspects of the services themselves when compared to the content available on them. The Digital Review aims to change that. We intend to provide you with news, articles and reviews on various digital media services with a consumer-oriented focus, initially focusing on services providing anime and manga and later expanding into other areas like music, movies, books and whatnot.

The site and its contents are still work in progress. Initially you can expect another article on Crunchyroll focusing on their recent developments and after that you should expect some reviews on various anime services. Ideas and suggestions are welcome - you can follow and tweet at us on Twitter for that. Welcome!
